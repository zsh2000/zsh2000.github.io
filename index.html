Shuhong Zheng

I'm a PhD student in computer science at University of Toronto, advised by Prof. Igor Gilitschenski. Prior to that, I obtained my M.S. in computer science at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign supervised by Prof. Yu-Xiong Wang, and my B.S. in computer science at Peking University supervised by Prof. Jiaying Liu and worked as an intern student in STRUCT Team.

My research interests mainly focus on computer vision, with experiences in scene understanding, generative models and computational photography.
