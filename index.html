Shuhong Zheng

I’m Shuhong Zheng and I’m now a MSCS student at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). Prior to that, I obtained my B.S. in computer science at Peking University (PKU), supervised by Prof. Jiaying Liu and worked as an intern student in STRUCT Team.

My research interests mainly focus on computer vision, with experiences in scene understanding, generative models and computational photography.
